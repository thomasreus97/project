   Shows extra information about the visualisations.
Year, location and occupancy can be manually selected above.

Map Info
Hover: Shows province name and amount of the current land occupany in km-squared.
Click: Updates piechart on the right for the clicked province.
Scroll: Zoom in and out.

Pie Info
Light color: Deselected occupancy in legend below and (stacked) barchart on the right.
Black border: Current selected occupancy in map on the left.
Hover: Shows land occupancy and it's area in km-squared of current province.
Click: Updates map on the left for the clicked occupany.
Click on light color: 'Click' + selects occupancy in legend and barchart.

Bar Info
Black border: Current selected occupancy in map on the left of the page.
Hover: Shows land occupancy and it's area in km-squared of the province on the x-axis.
Click: Updates map on the left of the page for the clicked occupany.

Legend Info
Light color: Deselected occupancy in (stacked) barchart on the right.
Click: (De)select occupancy and update (stacked) barchart.
